Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

The short version: Ride Forge stores all your workout data locally on your device only. We do not run servers, do not collect personal information, and do not serve advertisements or track your behaviour. Internet access is only used when you explicitly upload a workout to Strava.

1. Who We Are

Ride Forge is a smart trainer workout app developed by Ascenso. Questions about this policy can be sent to your-email@domain.com.

2. Data We Collect and Store

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's standard app storage. It is never transmitted to our servers — because we don't have any.

Data Purpose Where stored
Workout history (power, speed, duration, heart rate) Show past sessions in the app Device only
Connected Bluetooth device names Reconnect automatically Device only
App preferences (FTP, weight, settings) Personalise the experience Device only
Strava OAuth token (if you connect Strava) Authorise FIT file uploads to Strava Device only

When you uninstall the app, all of this data is deleted automatically by Android.

3. Bluetooth

The app uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to communicate with:

  • Smart trainers — to read power, cadence, speed, and send resistance commands
  • Heart rate monitors — to read heart rate

Bluetooth sensor data is used only within the app in real time and stored locally as part of your workout history. It is never shared with third parties unless you explicitly choose to upload a workout.

4. Internet and Third-Party Services

The app requests internet permission exclusively for the optional integrations below. No network requests are made without your direct action.

Strava (optional)

If you connect your Strava account and upload a workout, your workout FIT file is sent to Strava, Inc. via their official API. Strava's handling of your data is governed by Strava's Privacy Policy. You can disconnect Strava at any time in the app's Settings screen.

Feedback form (optional)

The app contains an optional link to a Google Form for submitting feedback. If you use it, Google's privacy policy applies. No data is pre-filled or automatically submitted.

5. Location Permission

Ride Forge requests the ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION permission on Android. This is not used for GPS or to determine your physical location. Android requires this permission for apps that scan for Bluetooth Low Energy devices. The app never reads, stores, or transmits your location.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party, except as described in Section 4 when you explicitly initiate an upload to Strava.

7. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a connected service (e.g. Strava), please contact that service's support team directly.

8. Data Deletion

All app data is stored on your device. To delete it:

  • Individual workouts — delete them from the workout history screen.
  • All app data — uninstall the app. Android removes all associated data automatically.
  • Strava connection — disconnect via Settings → Strava. This removes the stored token from your device. To revoke access from Strava's side, visit your Strava account settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. The current policy is always available at this URL.
